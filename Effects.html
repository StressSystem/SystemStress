Why are these such a problem for young people?

These may not seem too serious but in actuality, they pose a massive problem as they can greatly affect a young person's ability to learn, work, and engage socially.

Why is this a concern?

This is a serious concern because digital stress can harm both mental and physical health, especially in young people whose brains and emotional coping skills are still developing. It can lead to long-term issues like depression, anxiety, and academic struggles, and may even affect relationships and self-esteem. If left unaddressed, the impact can carry into adulthood.

Now to understand the effects of digital stress you first need to know what some of them are.

Some of the effects of digital stress in young people include:

  • Anxiety or panic attacks
  • Isolation or withdrawal from social activities
  • Increased secrecy
  • Anger
  • Depression
  • Failing grades
  • Rebellion
  • Stomachaches, headaches or other aches

What does it take to stop this?

Stopping or avoiding digital stress is fairly simple and can be done by just taking a break from time to time and doing something simple or repetitive so that your mind can relax and that way you can avoid being affected by digital stress.
