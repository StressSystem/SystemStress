Digital Stress

What is digital stress?

Digital stress is the feeling of pressure, anxiety, or overload from using phones, computers, and the internet. It can be caused by constant notifications, the need to respond quickly, social media comparison, or online conflict. People may feel anxious, have trouble sleeping, or feel tired after being online too much.
